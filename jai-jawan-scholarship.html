Jai Jawan Scholarship

Operation Sindoor, launched in response to the tragic attack on civilians in Pahalgam, reminded the Nation once again of the unwavering courage and sacrifice of our armed forces. In the wake of this, on 21st May, 2025, Honorable Founder Chancellor, LPU and Member Parliament (Rajya Sabha), Dr. Ashok Kumar Mittal proudly announced the Jai Jawan Scholarship — a heartfelt initiative offering 100% tuition fee waiver to active personnel of the Indian Armed Forces (Army, Navy, Air Force), Paramilitary (CRPF, ITBP, BSF, SSB, CISF, NSG, Assam Rifles) Forces & SPG enrolling in LPU Online undergraduate and postgraduate programmes.

Dr. Ashok Kumar Mittal mentioned, “No amount of salutes are ever enough to honour the bravery and sacrifices of our defence and paramilitary Forces.”

This scholarship is a meaningful gesture of national support, launched to honour the bravery and sacrifice of our armed forces after Operation Sindoor, where our soldiers responded quickly and bravely to the tragic attack on civilians in Pahalgam.

LPU believes that true respect must go beyond words — it must empower those who protect our Nation, through action.

Dr. Ashok Kumar Mittal

LLB, PhD (h.c.)
Founder Chancellor LPU
Member of Parliament (Rajya Sabha)

Who Is Eligible?

All currently serving personnel in:

Indian Army

Dedicated protectors of our land borders, upholding national security with unwavering courage.

Indian Navy

Guardians of our maritime frontiers, ensuring security and strength across the high seas.

Indian Air Force

Masters of the skies, defending our airspace with speed, precision, and excellence.

Paramilitary Personnel

Including CRPF, ITBP, BSF, SSB, CISF, NSG & Assam Rifles — brave hearts safeguarding our borders and maintaining internal security.

All ranks are covered — jawans and officers alike.

How to Apply?

To apply for this scholarship, please fill out the enquiry form on this page. Once we receive your interest, we will share another form link with you where you'll need to upload the documents listed below for eligibility verification.

Documents to be uploaded (all in a single ZIP file):

1. Proof of Active Service
(Any one from: Active Service Proof / Service Letter / Current Job Proof in Defence or Paramilitary Forces)

2. Scanned Passport-size Photograph

3. Scanned Signature

4. Aadhaar Card

5. Handwriting Sample

6. Academic Qualification Documents (Scanned copies of original documents only):

— For UG / Diploma Programs: 10th & 12th Mark sheets (DMCs) and Passing Certificates
— For PG Programs: 10th, 12th & Graduation Mark sheets (DMCs) and Degree Certificate

Jai Jawan Scholarship Terms & Conditions

Admission Eligibility

Applicants must meet the programme-specific eligibility criteria as prescribed by the University.

Document Verification

Scholarships will be awarded only after successful verification of the required documents. Applicants should send scanned copy of Original documents.
Failure to submit required documents within the stipulated time will lead to cancellation of the scholarship.

Fee Coverage

100% Tuition Fee waiver applicable on:
Application Fee
Programme Fee
Examination Fee
Degree and consolidated marksheet (upon completion of programme)
The Scholarship fully covers all programme-related expenses, with no additional charges applicable under normal circumstances. However, in the event that a candidate avails any extra services due to deviations from the academic calendar, non-adherence to progression timelines, failure in examination, change of mind regarding a chosen specialization, or has special requests like issuance of specially needed certificates, the applicable charges, if any, will need to be borne by the candidate. For e.g., fee for reappear examination, re-evaluation, programme transfer, change of specialization, etc.

Progression Requirements

Learners must appear for the End Term Examinations to be promoted to the next term.

Duration

Benefit of Scholarship is for the normal/minimum duration of the programme, with an extension of 6 months.
However, if a candidate takes more than normal/minimum duration to pass the programme, including the six months extension, he will not be entitled for the Scholarship benefits for any period beyond the prescribed duration.

Integrity and Conduct

Any case of misrepresentation, concealment, or false information will lead to cancellation of admission and scholarship.
Only learners who:
Have not faced disciplinary action
Have not been charged with academic misconduct or use of unfair means will remain eligible for scholarship continuation.

Final Authority

Admission and scholarship decisions rest solely with the competent authority of the University and are not claimable as a matter of right.

Uncovered Scenarios

In case of any matter not covered here and for interpretation of any content of this scholarship scheme, the decision of the competent authority of the University shall be final and binding on all concerned.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible for the Jai Jawan Scholarship?

Active personnel of Indian Armed Forces (Army, Navy, Air Force), Paramilitary Forces (CRPF, BSF, ITBP, CISF, SSB, Assam Rifles), and Special Protection Group (SPG) are eligible. The scholarship offers 100% tuition fee waiver on all UG and PG online programmes at LPU Online.

What does the Jai Jawan Scholarship cover?

The scholarship covers 100% tuition fee waiver across all undergraduate and postgraduate online programmes. Students only need to pay the one-time registration fee and semester examination fees.

How do I apply for the Jai Jawan Scholarship?

Simply fill out the scholarship application form on this page with your details, submit your valid military/paramilitary service ID for verification, select your preferred programme, and get enrolled with the 100% tuition fee waiver.

Can retired defence personnel apply for this scholarship?

The Jai Jawan Scholarship is specifically designed for active/serving personnel of the Indian Armed Forces and Paramilitary Forces. Retired personnel are encouraged to contact the LPU Online admissions office at 93113 81814 for information about other available scholarships and benefits.

What documents are required for verification?

Applicants need to submit a valid military/paramilitary service identity card, academic qualification documents (mark sheets and certificates), passport-sized photographs, and an Aadhaar card for identity verification.

Can I pursue the programme while being posted at remote locations?

Yes! LPU Online programmes are designed for maximum flexibility. LIVE classes are recorded and available 24/7, exams are conducted online, and the mobile app provides on-the-go access. You can study from any location with an internet connection.
