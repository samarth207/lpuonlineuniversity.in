Frequently Asked Questions

Is LPU Online degree valid and UGC recognised?

Yes, LPU Online degrees are entitled by UGC-DEB (University Grants Commission - Distance Education Bureau). The degrees issued are exactly the same as on-campus degrees from Lovely Professional University, with no mention of 'online' or 'distance' on the degree certificate. The degree holds the same value as a regular on-campus degree for jobs, higher studies, and competitive exams.

What online programs does LPU Online offer?

LPU Online offers a wide range of UGC entitled programs including MBA (Master of Business Administration), MCA (Master of Computer Applications), M.Com (Master of Commerce), M.Sc in Mathematics & Economics, MA in English, Political Science, History & Sociology, BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration), BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications), BA (Bachelor of Arts), and Diploma programs in Business Administration and Computer Applications.

What is the admission process for LPU Online?

The admission process is simple and fully online: 1) Fill out the online application form on the website, 2) Submit your required academic documents for verification, 3) Pay the admission fee (No Cost EMI options are available), 4) Get enrolled and access the Learning Management System (LMS) portal immediately. No entrance exam is required for any program.

How are exams conducted in LPU Online?

LPU Online conducts online proctored exams that are monitored LIVE through AI-powered tools. Students can draw their own datesheet based on personal preferences and appear for exams from the comfort of their home. The entire exam process is designed to be hassle-free, flexible, and secure.

Does LPU Online provide placement support?

Yes, LPU Online provides comprehensive placement support including personalised career counselling, resume building workshops, mock interview sessions, industry readiness programs, and access to 450+ top hiring partners. LPU has a proven placement track record with top recruiters like Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Deloitte, TCS, Infosys, and many more.

What is the fee structure for LPU Online programs?

LPU Online offers highly affordable and competitive fee structures with No Cost EMI options available across all programs. The fees vary by program and specialization. For detailed program-wise fee information, contact the admissions team at 93113 81814 or fill out the enquiry form on this page.

Can working professionals pursue LPU Online programs?

Absolutely! LPU Online programs are specifically designed for working professionals. LIVE interactive classes are held on weekends, 400+ hours of recorded content is available for anytime-anywhere access, and exams are conducted online from home. Students can learn at their own pace without disrupting their professional careers or daily routines.

What is the Jai Jawan Scholarship at LPU Online?

The Jai Jawan Scholarship is a special initiative by LPU Online that offers 100% tuition fee waiver to active personnel of Indian Armed Forces (Army, Navy, Air Force) and Paramilitary Forces (CRPF, BSF, ITBP, CISF, SSB, Assam Rifles) & SPG. It covers all UG and PG online programmes. Learn more about the Jai Jawan Scholarship.
